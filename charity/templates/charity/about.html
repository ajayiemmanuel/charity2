Chinedu Okoli

Founder

Chinedu Okoli is the Founder and President of The Chinedu Okoli Foundation. Born in Enugu, hailing from Anambra State, Chinedu Okoli popularly known as Flavour N’abania or more recently, the ‘Flavour of Africa’ is recognized as Africa’s Highlife King. Having captivated audiences with his unique and eclectic sound, Flavour has set the standard in his field and still has a lot more to offer. Established in 2010 and based in Lagos, Nigeria, The Chinedu Okoli Foundation aims to provide young people with the chance to experience the impact of music through education and empowerment. As a United Nations Peace Ambassador, non-profit leader, musician and mentor, Chinedu Okoli has been serving individuals and families living in poverty in Nigeria, Liberia, and other parts of Africa since 2010. Chinedu Okoli continues to leverage his global philanthropic experiences and grassroots efforts, in order to advance economic mobility throughout Africa. As President of The Chinedu Okoli Foundation, he is fulfilling his passion for empowering underserved individuals to overcome poverty and fulfill their economic potential. He achieves this by combining his passion for music and genuine love for humanity. Simply put, Chinedu Okoli believes that “CHARITY BEGINS AT HOME” and this is seen through the tireless efforts that The Chinedu Okoli Foundation is committed to throughout Africa.
